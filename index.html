My Journey

From fitness studio
to functional medicine

I'm a dedicated fitness professional with over 20 years of experience in the health and wellness industry. Based in Las Vegas, NV, I've had the privilege of owning and operating my very own fitness studio, where I helped countless individuals achieve their fitness goals and lead healthier lives.

Over the years, I trained clients and participated in numerous fitness modeling and competition events. As I got older and my clients' needs evolved, I went back to school to earn my second Bachelor's degree in Nutrition. It was during this time that I realized nutrition is everything — nutrition is medicine. So I couldn't stop there; I then completed a Master's in Human Nutrition and Functional Medicine.

"I try to have my clients make their cake and eat it too!"

As a Functional Medicine Nutritionist, I provide a personalized, root-cause approach to health and wellness. I work with clients to uncover and address the underlying factors contributing to their symptoms, using nutrition as a key pillar of healing.

I specialize in helping clients with:

  • Inflammation and chronic pain management
  • Food allergies and sensitivities
  • Gut health and digestion, including detoxification
  • Hormone imbalances, such as PCOS, thyroid, and adrenal issues
  • Weight management and metabolic health
